Why "Netochka"?

  • Netochka Nezvanova is a wonderful, if little-known and unfinished, novel by Dostoevsky that deserves more attention (IndieBound Link).
  • Netochka (an affectionate form of the name "Anna") is close enough in Russian to "Anonymous" as to fit an online pseudonym.
